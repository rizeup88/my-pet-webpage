Estrella, A Golden Retriever You'll Love

If you ever wanted a Golden Retriever, this will tell you all you need to know

Perks

  • Golden Retrievers are excellent at being dogs that relieve stress, and keep their owners active.
  • They are especially recommended as a breed for seeing-eye dogs for those hard of sight.
  • They take direction in a quick timeframe, and are friendly around children.

What makes Estrella unique?

  • She can recognize commands in both English and Spanish.
  • She can sense your stress, and is quick to approach you to provide a sense of calm by petting her.
  • Her diet is a fair combination of pellet-based food, and raw meat, which makes it easy to share a meal together.
  • She prefers fetching rawhides, as opposed to balls or sticks.

Final thoughts

When considering pets that are faithful to their owners, and are dependable when you need them most, look no further than a dog. That's exactly why my Golden Estrella is both a best friend, and a lifelong companion my family and I have come to love."
